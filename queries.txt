Search Queries to Set Up Alerts For

Platforms searched via SerpAPI: Google Jobs (aggregates Indeed, Glassdoor, LinkedIn, Ladders, ZipRecruiter, and dozens more)
Supplementary via WebSearch: Greenhouse, Lever direct ATS searches, company careers pages

## Instructions
# Customize these queries for YOUR target roles, industries, and locations.
# The search agent rotates through ~12 queries per day.
# SerpAPI Google Jobs works best with simple title-based queries.
# Use OR to combine related titles in one query.
# Location filtering is handled by the SerpAPI location parameter, not the query string.
